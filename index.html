FAQ

Answers for curious creators.

Which chains does HeliosOn support?

We’re live on Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, and more. Additional chains can be enabled on request.

How do you prevent Sybil attacks?

We combine on-chain signatures, device fingerprinting, and behavioral scoring to catch fraudulent accounts before rewards are issued.

Can I import existing quests?

Yes. Use our CSV importer or API to bring in historic quest data and immediately continue tracking progress.

Does HeliosOn provide analytics?

Absolutely. Dashboards provide live funnels, cohort retention, and conversion insights tailored to your KPI targets.
